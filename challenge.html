Food For All DC

Fall 2024

Established in 1984, Food For All CD delivers pre-packaged groceries to homebound/non-homebound food insecure persons in the DC area.

  • Serving over 1,000 families monthly
  • Partnering with 20+ local organizations
  • Driven by a dedicated team of volunteers
